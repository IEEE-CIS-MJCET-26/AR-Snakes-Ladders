TrailBlaze AR

Print this page, cut out the markers, and place them around your space to play!

A
Checkpoint A – Alpha Station
Preset: HIRO (built-in AR.js)
🔢 Digit Only – gives you: 7
What happens: Shows digit "7" floating above the marker.
Digit is collected into your clue bank.
B
Checkpoint B – Beta Crossing
Preset: KANJI (built-in AR.js)
🧭 Arrow Only – points toward 120°
What happens: Shows a yellow arrow that dynamically rotates as you turn your phone, pointing toward 120° (ESE direction to Marker C).
C
Checkpoint C – Charlie Peak
Pattern: LETTER-A (custom)
✨ Arrow + Digit – gives you: 3
⚠️ Demo tip: For this custom marker, use the 🎭 Simulate Marker button in the app instead.
What happens: Shows a purple arrow pointing toward 200° (SSW to Marker D) + digit "3" floating alongside.
D
Checkpoint D – Delta Ridge
Pattern: LETTER-B (custom)
🔥 Arrow Only – points toward 300°
What happens: Shows an orange arrow pointing toward 300° (WNW) toward the final Marker E.
E
Checkpoint E – Echo Summit 🏆
Pattern: LETTER-C (custom)
🏆 FINAL – Arrow + Digit – gives you: 9
What happens: Shows a pink arrow (spins – this is the end!) + digit "9".
After detecting this marker, the Victory screen appears with your collected code: 7 • 3 • 9

📋 How to Play

1

Print this page (use your browser's Print function). Cut out the 5 marker cards.

2

Place markers around your home or space, roughly in the directions described (or anywhere for testing).

3

Open index.html on your phone using a local dev server (or ngrok for HTTPS). Tap Launch AR Hunt.

4

Point your phone camera at a printed marker. Marker A (HIRO) and Marker B (KANJI) work out of the box!

5

Watch the arrow rotate as you physically turn your phone. Follow it to the next marker.

6

No physical markers? Use the 🎭 Simulate Marker button in the app to test all 5 checkpoints.

🔑 Secret Code: Collect all 3 digits (from Markers A, C and E) to reveal the treasure code: 7 – 3 – 9
