📋 How to Play

1

Print this page (use your browser's Print function). Cut out the 8 marker cards.

2

Place markers around your home or space, roughly in the directions described (or anywhere for testing).

3

Open index.html on your phone using a local dev server (or ngrok for HTTPS). Tap Launch AR Hunt.

4

Point your phone camera at a printed marker. This route uses barcode values 0 through 7 in sequence.

5

Watch the arrow rotate as you physically turn your phone. Follow it to the next marker.

6

No physical markers? Use the 🎭 Simulate Marker button in the app to test all 8 checkpoints.

🔑 Secret Code: Collect all 4 digits (from Markers A, C, F and H) to reveal the treasure code: 7 – 3 – 4 – 8
